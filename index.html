Make sure Fractal Scribe is running on your Mac, then enter your Mac's address and tap Connect.

Privacy: Notes are written directly to your Mac — no OneDrive, no Microsoft, no third parties. The only external service is the Anthropic API call to read your handwriting.

Tip: Scan the QR code shown on your Mac to fill this address automatically.

Privacy: Your API key is stored only in this browser session and never sent anywhere except directly to Anthropic's API. Each upload is a fresh call with no session memory. Photo metadata (GPS, device info) is stripped before any image leaves your device.
Obsidian Vault

Enter your OneDrive personal access token and vault folder path, then click Connect.

How to get your token: Go to Graph Explorer, sign in with your Microsoft account, and copy the access token from the Access Token tab. It lasts about 60 minutes — you'll need to refresh it each session.

What gets sent to Anthropic: Your new photos (metadata stripped) + note titles and tags from your vault only. Full note content stays on OneDrive and is never sent.
